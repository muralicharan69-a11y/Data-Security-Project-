EchoCrypt

Secure Voice Communication with Audio Steganography and Fingerprint Authentication

Hide AES-encrypted messages inside short voice recordings. The receiver authenticates with a simulated fingerprint before extracting and decrypting the secret.

Go to Sender Go to Receiver

How it works

  1. Record a short voice clip on the sender page.
  2. Encrypt your secret message with AES-EAX.
  3. Embed the ciphertext into the audio's least significant bits.
  4. Send the encoded WAV to the receiver.
  5. Authenticate with the demo fingerprint password.
  6. Extract & decrypt the original message.
